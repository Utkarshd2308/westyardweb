Short Description

Indulge in the wholesome, natural sweetness of Jaggery by Westyard Agro, a traditional unrefined sugar packed with rich, earthy flavor and nutritional benefits. Made from premium sugarcane juice, this golden-brown sweetener retains its natural minerals like iron, potassium, and magnesium, making it a healthier alternative to refined sugar. Ideal for sweetening beverages, baking, and preparing authentic Indian desserts, jaggery adds depth and complexity to both sweet and savory dishes. With its distinctive caramel-like taste and natural, minimally processed nature, Westyard Agro’s jaggery is a must-have for those seeking a touch of tradition in their pantry. Store in a cool, dry place to maintain its freshness and quality.
